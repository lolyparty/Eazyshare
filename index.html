About

Eazyshare app defies the odds of using wifi, bluetooth and cellular network technology to connect to other devices. With the pick and drop method of sharing, Eazyshare is second to none. It saves you quality time by sharing apps, music, videos and files with friends and families in the blink of an eye.

How it works

Pick

When you have decided on the app, music, video or file that you want to share, pick it from your Eazyshare directory...

Drop

Drop the app, music, video or file into the Eazyshare directory of a destination device without any friction in between.
