About

Eazyshare app defies the odds of using wifi, bluetooth and cellular network technology to connect to other devices such as android, ios, windows and macOs. With the pick and drop method of sharing, Eazyshare is second to none. It saves you quality time by sharing apps, music, videos and files to you loved ones in the blink of an eye. Its unlimited sharing volume comes in handy, as it does it in record time.

Drop the file
